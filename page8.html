What Is Key Club?

Key Club is one of the largest student-led service organizations with over 251,000 members across 40 countries. Our aim to teach leadership and build character through acts of service while upholding Key Club's four core values: caring, leadership, inclusiveness, and character building.

PLEDGE
I pledge, on my honor,
to uphold the Objects of Key Club International;
to build my home, school and community;
to serve my nation and world;
and combat all forces which tend to undermine these institutions.

Core Values

Leadership

Leadership is grounded in humility and service to others. A true leader listens, recognizes, and empowers.

Caring

Character is built by everyday acts of kindness, integrity and teamwork to grow stronger relationships and communities.

Mission Statement
Key Club is an international, student-led organization that provides its members with opportunities to provide service, build character and develop leadership

Motto
Caring- Our way of life.

Objectives

1

Primacy

To give primacy to the human and spiritual, rather than to the material values of life.
2

Encourage

To encourage the daily living of the Golden Rule in all human relationships.
3

Promote

To promote the adoption and application of higher standards in scholarship, sportsmanship and social contacts
4

Develop

To develop, by precept and example, a more intelligent, aggressive and serviceable citizenship.
5

Provide

To provide a practical means to form enduring friendships, to render unselfish service and to build better communities.
6

Cooperate

To cooperate in creating and maintaining that sound public opinion and high idealism which makes possible the increase of righteousness, justice, patriotism and good will.
